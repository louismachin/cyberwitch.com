Do we use cookies?

Yes, but you can disable or delete them if you wish. (Cookies are small files that a site or it's service providers send to your web browser. You browser will retain these files on your hard disk for a limited time if space is available. You can adjust your browser settings to control these behaviors and you're free to delete your cookies at any time. If you choose to allow your browser to return a cookie that's been sent to you, a web site may be able to associate your current visit with a prior visit to that site, or in the case of third party cookies, to other another website which the third party serves. In addition, cookies can store and transmit small amounts of information stored by a website or third party website. Modern browsers allow you to reject third party cookies while accepting and returning the cookies of the website you're visiting. Rejecting third party cookies provides better privacy protection while you're browsing the web).
